Introducing Real-Time Content Updates

October 20, 2023
David Kimani

We're excited to announce a major upgrade to Qybrr's content system! Starting today, our platform will update every 30 minutes with fresh content from our sources across Kenya and East Africa.

This means you'll always have access to the latest news and stories as they develop. Our automated system now efficiently aggregates content from a wide range of reliable sources, ensuring you stay informed about everything from business developments to tech innovations.

Key Improvements

  • Increased update frequency from 12 hours to 30 minutes
  • Enhanced error handling for more reliable content retrieval
  • Improved SSL verification to support more source websites
  • Better handling of relative URLs and image paths

We've also streamlined our content selection to show just two key stories from each category, helping you focus on what matters most without information overload.

"Our goal is to deliver the most relevant, up-to-date content in a clean, distraction-free environment. These updates bring us one step closer to that vision." — David Kimani, Lead Developer

Today, we're thrilled to announce that Qybrr has significantly expanded its content sources to include several new Kenyan and East African publications.

These partnerships allow us to provide more diverse, reliable content while maintaining our commitment to providing a clean, ad-free reading experience.

The Story Behind Qybrr's Minimalist Design

October 5, 2023
Aisha Omondi

In a digital world filled with distractions, pop-ups, and intrusive ads, we wanted to create something different. Qybrr's design philosophy centers on putting content first and allowing readers to focus on what matters.

When we began designing Qybrr, we took inspiration from both modern digital publications and classic print newspapers. We wanted to combine the clean typography and focused layout of print with the interactivity and accessibility of digital.

Design Principles

Our key design principles include:

  • Content First: Minimizing UI elements that don't directly serve the content
  • Readable Typography: Optimal font sizes, line heights, and contrast
  • Purposeful White Space: Giving content room to breathe
  • Subtle Animations: Providing feedback without distraction
  • Accessibility: Ensuring content is available to all users

The result is a reading experience that feels calm, focused, and respectful of your time and attention. We believe this approach helps our readers stay better informed without the cognitive load that comes with cluttered designs.

We're continuously refining our design based on user feedback and the latest best practices in web accessibility and user experience. We'd love to hear your thoughts on our approach!
